# Financial Targets & KPIs

## Core Financial Target
- **Food Cost (FC)**: ≤ 30%
- Achieved through: zero-waste production, standardized "bricks", CBS (converts low-cost raw materials into high-perceived-value items)

## Cost Structure
- **L1 Rent**: 18,000 THB/month (paid for 1 year)
- **L2 Rent**: 20,000 THB/month (paid for 6 months)
- **Staff**: 4-6 people

## Revenue Model
- **Primary**: L2 at Tops Daily — salad bar + grab & go + hot meals
- **Secondary** (Phase 2): L1 mini-store — roadside sales, bottled sauces, sourdough
- **Future** (Phase 3): Yoga mornings + seating at L1 3rd floor
- **Split**: 80% take-away, 20% eat-in

## Operational KPIs
- **Time to Receipt (TTR)**: Target <2 min at L2
- **Delivery cycles**: 3x/day from L1 to L2
- **Batch prep window**: 07:00-10:00 daily
- **Blast Chiller cycle**: <90 min from +85C to +3C

## Growth Phases
1. Phase 1: Prove the model at Rawai (L1+L2)
2. Phase 2: Expand L1 to mini-store + optimize operations
3. Phase 3: Add seating + yoga + explore franchise potential

## Zero-Waste Economics
- Salmon trimmings → tartare / poke
- Bones and trimmings → broth (chicken ramen base)
- Herb stems, lemon rinds, vegetable peels → CBS Boosters (Green Gold Oil, Shishka Dust)
- Result: higher margin on "waste" products than on original ingredients
