# Benchmarks — Competitor Analysis

> **Purpose**: Capture comparable brands and extract what to adopt and what to avoid.

## Benchmark Table

| Name | Country | Description | Similarity | What to Adopt |
|------|---------|-------------|------------|---------------|
| **Salata** | USA (South-East, TX/FL) | Fast-casual salad kitchen, 60+ toppings, 12 dressings, 100+ locations, franchise | HIGH: Custom salad bar, unlimited combos, healthy low-cal, similar to our MVP | Customizable toppings (our 20-56), franchise model for growth, supermarket locations (like our L2 at Tops) |
| **Salatoush** | UAE (Dubai, Abu Dhabi) | Salad bar with superfood/keto/vegan, custom bowls, plant proteins, small chain | HIGH: Custom salad + healthy twists (superfoods), Arab influence, salad bar custom | Superfood emphasis, adapt sauerkraut/kimchi for Middle Eastern palate, small chain Instagram-vibe focus |
| **Saladworks** | USA (East Coast, 100+ loc) | Custom salads/wraps/grain bowls, 60+ ingredients, franchise, supermarket partnerships | HIGH: Salad bar + fresh toppings, supermarket integrations (like L2 at Tops) | Partnerships (like Shishka at Tops for traffic), grain bowls, our fusion approach as differentiator |
| **Just Salad** | USA (NYC, 7 states, 100+ loc) | Custom salads/bowls/wraps, AI personalization, reusable, sustainable | HIGH: Custom bar + AI recommendations (similar to our AI nutrient tool) | AI integration for nutrition tool, sustainable brand, expansion playbook at scale |
| **Sweetgreen** | USA (DC origin, nationwide, 225+ loc) | Salads/grain bowls, farm-sourced, seasonal, robot automation | HIGH: Salad/bar with healthy seasonal, fusion elements | Farm-to-table sourcing, superfood seasonal bowls, L1/L2 efficiency via automation, dinner expansion + yoga phase |
| **Chopt** | USA (NYC, East Coast, 100+ loc) | Fast-casual 75% plant-based, custom salads/bowls/wraps, kiosks | HIGH: Chopped custom salads with bold flavors, healthy focus | Plant-based (75%) focus, kiosk/POS efficiency, growth model |
| **Diced** | USA (FL/Raleigh, 4 loc) | Bowls/salads/wraps/smoothies, chopped style, fresh prep, startup | HIGH: Custom chopped salads, healthy trust slogan, small startup origin | Chopped method, assembly-line approach, healthy budget positioning for Rawai |
| **Naked Farmer** | USA (FL, Tampa/Miami, 5 loc) | Farm-to-table bowls/plates, local-sourced, seasonal | MEDIUM: Custom plates with healthy local focus, fusion | Local sourcing for our coconut/superfood network, chain model |
| **Giardino Gourmet Salads** | USA (FL South, 10+ loc) | Gourmet custom salads/wraps/bowls, fresh garden-to-bowl, franchise | HIGH: Custom salad concept with healthy integrity, small franchise | Garden-to-bowl philosophy (aligns with Flower of Life brand), franchise at small scale, gourmet positioning |
| **Panera Bread** | USA (nationwide, 2000+ loc) | Fast-casual bakery-cafe, salads/sandwiches/soups, custom | MEDIUM: Salad/soup custom, healthy bakery fusion | Bakery integration (our sourdough), variety for national scaling |
| **Tender Greens** | USA (CA, West Coast, 30+ loc) | Chef-led farm-to-table salads/bowls, seasonal, sustainable | HIGH: Custom healthy bowls, sustainable | Chef-inspired seasonal bowls, sustainability analysis for growth |
| **The Salad Bar** | USA (various, LLC) | Custom salad bar with fresh combos, rewards program, small chain | HIGH: Pure salad bar concept with custom | Rewards/loyalty program (plus matching our salad bar model) |
| **Subway** | Global (37,000+ loc) | Custom sandwiches/salads, healthy options, made-to-order, franchise-heavy | MEDIUM: Build-your-own (like salad bar), grab&go, fusion | Franchise scalability, our goal (FC ≤30%), standardization |

## Key Insights
1. **Salad bar customization** is the dominant trend — our 56-container approach is competitive
2. **AI/tech integration** (Just Salad, Sweetgreen) validates our AI nutrition tool direction
3. **Superfoods + fermentation** is under-exploited — our CBS and fermented products are genuine differentiators
4. **Arab-Russian fusion** has NO direct competitor in SE Asia — unique positioning
5. **Supermarket partnerships** (Saladworks, Salata) validate our L2-at-Tops model
