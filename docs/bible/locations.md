# Locations & Phased Development

## L1 (Kitchen + Mini-Store)
- **Size**: 25-30 m2, 2 floors (balcony with mountain view)
- **Rent**: 18,000 THB/month (paid for 1 year)
- **Location**: Rawai, Phuket

### Development Phases
- **Phase 1**: Prep kitchen only (cutting, cooking, bottled sauces, sourdough bread)
- **Phase 2**: Open mini-store with outdoor seating (roadside) and 2nd-floor seating (mountain view)
- **Phase 3**: Add 3rd floor with seating + morning yoga classes led by general manager. Work permits in process

## L2 (Sales Point at Tops Daily)
- **Size**: 32 m2
- **Rent**: 20,000 THB/month (paid for 6 months)
- **Location**: High-traffic location, ~1km from L1

### Layout
- Core: Two 1.5m salad bars (56 trays of varying sizes) with LED lighting for vibrant "ingredient path"
- Assembly on-site (<2 min)
- Reheat for wraps/sandwiches
- 2-4 tables
- Open fridge (1.2m x 2m) at hall end for take-away (soups, coconuts, mashed potatoes with cutlet)

## Phases of Development (Global)
1. **Phase 1** (March 2026): L2 as main store + L1 as kitchen
2. **Phase 2**: L1 mini-store with roadside / 2nd-floor seating
3. **Phase 3**: L1 3rd floor (seating + yoga mornings; permits in process)
