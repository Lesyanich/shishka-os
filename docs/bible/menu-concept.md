# Menu Concept & Culinary Architecture

## Production Model: L1 (The Factory) → L2 (The Assembly Line)

### L1 — Production (Motto: "Precision & Preservation")
- Sear proteins for Maillard reaction, stop cooking before core is fully set ("90% Cooked" rule)
- Blast Chiller: +85C → +3C in <90 mins — locks moisture in protein fibers
- Chamber Vacuum Sealer: removes oxygen, prevents oxidation, extends shelf life 7-10 days
- Output: standardized, safe, nutrient-dense "bricks" ready for transport

### The Freeze-Frame (Shock + Vac)
- Transport strictly at +2...+4C in isothermal boxes
- Working stock (3-5 days shelf life) + emergency stock (30 days) for high-risk items

### L2 — Logistics: Cold Chain
- Inventory types: Fresh, Chilled, Cooked, Frozen
- Shift Leader checks temperature on arrival. If temp > +8C → batch rejected (Safety Protocol)

### L2 — Service (Motto: "Speed & Crunch")
- Handles ready-to-eat or ready-to-heat ingredients only
- Zero cross-contamination risk (No Raw Zone)
- High-Speed Regeneration: Merrychef/TurboChef ovens (NOT standard microwave)
- "90% Cooked" proteins from vacuum bag → Oven → Plate in ~60 seconds
- The oven evaporates surface moisture, restoring "grilled texture" while keeping inside juicy

## Food Cost Target
**FC ≤ 30%** through:
- Zero-waste production (salmon trimmings → tartare, bones → broth)
- Complete elimination of human factor at L2 assembly
- 100% consistent restaurant quality via standardized "bricks"

## Culinary Booster System (CBS)

### Overview
The CBS is a strategic "Flavor LEGO" framework. Instead of complex, labor-intensive sauces made on-site, we use concentrated "Boosters" produced at L1 Central Kitchen. Engineered to hit specific physiological triggers (Umami, Acid, Crunch). Delivered to L2 as ready-to-use, portion-controlled units.

### The 3-Axis Booster Model

**Axis A: The Foundation (Acid / Fat / Salt)**
- Purpose: Balance the palate and carry flavors
- Examples: Herb-infused cold-pressed oils (fat), fermented citrus vinegars (acid), mineral-rich sea salt blends

**Axis B: The Accents (Aroma / Umami)**
- Purpose: Provide depth and "craveability" without MSG or sugar
- Examples: Miso-Tomato "Umami Bombs", dehydrated mushroom dust, charred onion oils

**Axis C: The Finish (Texture / Heat / Sweet)**
- Purpose: Provide the "Emotional Response" (Crunch and Kick)
- Examples: Superfood Crunch-Mix (seeds/nuts/spices), house-made chili-crunch (low oil), citrus-zest dust

### Strategic Advantages (COO Reality Check)
- **Consistency**: 90% of flavor is "pre-locked" in the booster. L2 staff only assemble, don't "cook"
- **Zero-Waste**: Uses high-flavor scraps (lemon rinds, herb stems, vegetable peels) for premium powders and oils
- **Speed (TTR)**: Reduces "Time to Receipt" by 30-45 seconds per bowl
- **Food Cost (FC)**: Converts low-cost raw materials (seeds, tomato paste, local herbs) into high-perceived-value additives
- **Trend Fit**: Aligns with 2026 Gut Health & Fibermaxxing (ferments and seed-based textures)

### CBS Workflow: L1 Production → L2 Assembly
1. **L1 Extraction**: Using L1-OVN-01 (Convection) for dehydration and L1-BCH-01 (Blast Freezer) for preserving aromatic volatile oils in "Flavor Cubes"
2. **Portioning**: Boosters are vacuum-sealed or frozen in 10g/20g portions (Standardized SKU)
3. **L2 Finalization**:
   - Cold Bowls: squeeze of "Citrus-Herb Oil" + sprinkle of "Shishka Crunch"
   - Hot Proteins: single "Umami Cube" melted over grilled protein during final 30 seconds of heating

### Core Booster Menu (Phase 1)
- **"Green Gold" Oil**: Cold-pressed oil emulsified with cilantro/parsley stems and lime zest (Foundation/Fat)
- **"Shishka Dust"**: Dehydrated fermented vegetables (kimchi/sauerkraut scraps) ground into fine powder (Accent/Umami/Acid)
- **"Ancient Crunch"**: Toasted pumpkin seeds, black sesame, and sumac — Arab-Russian Fusion influence (Finish/Texture)

### Operational Requirements
- Equipment: Fully compatible with existing L1 infrastructure. No additional CapEx beyond minor smallwares (silicone molds, high-speed blender)
- Staffing: Requires 1 dedicated prep-chef at L1 for "Booster Days" (Batch production)
- Shelf Life: 80% of boosters designed for 30+ day shelf life (Frozen or Dehydrated)
