# Operations & People — Domain Context

## Scope
Staff scheduling, shift management, SOPs, training, equipment maintenance, hygiene protocols, kitchen workflow optimization.

## Key Systems
- **Admin Panel**: Schedule Manager, Kitchen Dashboard, Equipment management
- **Supabase**: `staff`, `shifts`, `equipment`, `equipment_maintenance`

## Team Structure
- Owner (Lesia) — strategy, finance, marketing
- Head Chef — kitchen operations, R&D, quality
- Kitchen staff — prep, cooking, plating, cleaning

## Typical Tasks
- Create/update SOP for a procedure
- Plan staff training session
- Schedule equipment maintenance
- Optimize prep workflow (reduce waste, save time)
- Update hygiene checklists
- Onboard new team member
- Resolve shift coverage gaps

## Equipment
- Equipment registry in Supabase
- Maintenance tracking (preventive + reactive)
- Location mapping (which equipment where)
- Manuals: `01_Business/Equipment_Manuals/`

## Standards
- Food safety: Thai FDA compliance
- Hygiene: daily cleaning logs
- Cross-contamination: separate boards for allergens
